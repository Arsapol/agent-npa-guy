## Evaluation Framework Applied

For each pick I asked:
1. What is this area BECOMING? (infrastructure pipeline confirmed vs speculative)
2. Can I DEVELOP this? (plot size, zoning, road access)
3. Holding cost: land tax + 7% equity opportunity cost
4. Title clean? (โฉนด only, road access confirmed)
5. Surroundings: industrial estate adjacency, demand anchors
