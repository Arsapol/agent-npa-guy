## 🎯 FINAL RECOMMENDATION

### #1 CHEWATHAI RAMKHAMHAENG — BUY (especially 3A0916 or 8Z6086)
The 50%+ discount makes this an outstanding value play. Even with ฿12K/mo rent to students, you get 6.5% net yield with MASSIVE capital upside. The 2-bed 60sqm layout is perfect for student sharing. ABAC is walkable-ish at 1km. The Orange Line future station nearby adds upside.

### #2 INSPIRE PLACE — CONDITIONAL (only if ABAC walkable matters)
You're paying market price, so the "NPA deal" aspect is zero. The saving grace is 3-min walk to ABAC — international students from ABAC may pay premium rent. But you could negotiate a regular resale unit at similar price without auction hassle.

### #3 BANGKOK HORIZON — PASS
Tiny discount, not walkable to any university, 1-bed only, mediocre yield. The transit access is nice but doesn't help the student rental thesis.
