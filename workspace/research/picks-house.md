## Methodology

Selection criteria applied:
1. District with LED sell-through >= 5% (proven demand signal)
2. Price ฿500K–5M (mortgage-friendly)
3. Real discount vs. area LED avg sold price (NOT appraisal)
4. Chanote deed (โฉนดเลขที่) only — excluded น.ส.3
5. GPS coordinates required (BAM/KBank picks)
6. Bedroom >= 2 (family market preference)
7. Excluded NHA government housing projects (social housing, not investment grade)

**LED benchmark context:**
