# NPA Condo Market Verification — Near International Schools Bangkok
**Date:** 2026-04-04
**Purpose:** Verify NPA prices against real market data for properties near international schools

---

## Methodology
- Market prices sourced from DDProperty, Hipflat, FazWaz (searches conducted 2026-04-04)
- USD figures converted at ฿35.5/USD (approximate rate)
- Yield calculated on NPA price (acquisition cost basis)
- Discount = (market_sqm − NPA_sqm) / market_sqm × 100
- Verdicts: STRONG BUY (>30% discount, >6% yield), BUY (20-30% discount, >5% yield), WATCH (10-20% discount), AVOID (<10% discount or <4% yield)

- **Developer:** Not confirmed
- **Listings:** 4 units listed for sale as of late 2025

Wells International (On Nut) serves international families — smaller school, less expat density than NIST/Bangkok Patana. Off-BTS location (Srinakarin 31) limits rental demand to families with cars. ## Key Findings

### Top Picks
1. **ซิตี้ โฮม ศรีนครินทร์** (฿911K) — best absolute value: 26.9% discount, 7.9% yield, Supalai developer, 14-year-old building, Concordian proximity. Low entry reduces downside significantly.
2. **นิช ไอดี ปากเกร็ด** (฿1.66M) — cleanest risk profile: Sena developer, modern ~10-year building, ISB proximity, 13.3% discount, 5.4% yield. ISB is top-tier expat school = sustained rental demand.

### Avoid
- **แอท ซิตี้ สุขุมวิท 101/1**: NPA price at or above transacted market — no NPA advantage
- **มายคอนโด สุขุมวิท 103**: 9.5% discount insufficient for NPA risk premium

### Needs More DD
- **สราญใจ แมนชั่น**: 1991 building — check sinking fund status and structural report before proceeding despite good yield
- **บ้านฝัน คอนโดวิลล์ (฿549K units)**: Extreme discount likely reflects damage/title issues — mandatory physical inspection
- **มาสเตอร์วิว เอ็กเซ็กคิวทีฟ**: Unit size from KBank must be confirmed — verdict swings 16.8% discount to premium based on sqm
- **ลุมพินี เมกะซิตี้ บางนา**: Unit size unknown + market declining -13.3% YTD — monitor

### Market Context
- Bangna/Samut Prakan corridor showing price softness (-13% YTD Jan 2025) — increased bargaining power but lower resale liquidity
- NIST/Sukhumvit corridor (สราญใจ) remains most liquid expat rental market
- ISB/Pak Kret cluster (Niche ID) has proven expat rental track record
- KIS International on Pracha Uthit is niche demand — Thai-Korean community, less liquid on resale
