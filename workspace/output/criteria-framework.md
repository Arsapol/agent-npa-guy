## Gate 1: Auto-Reject (any one = walk away)

| # | Rule | Source |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Leasehold < 30 years remaining | Risk |
| 2 | Invalid title deed (นส.3 underlying land) | Risk |
| 3 | Active structural notice from กรมโยธาธิการ | Risk |
| 4 | NPA price ≥ market price (zero/negative discount vs DDProperty/Hipflat) | All 4 |
| 5 | Building age > 20 years (pre-2006) | Flipper + Market |
| 6 | NPA concentration > 8% of total units in same building | Market |
| 7 | Unit size < 22 sqm or > 50 sqm (university); < 45 sqm or > 120 sqm (school/family) | Flipper + Rental |

## Gate 4: Pre-Purchase Due Diligence

| Check | Budget if Fail |
|---|---|
| Juristic fund solvency (≥ 70% funded) | Special assessment: 15-50K/unit |
| Juristic fee arrears (request หนังสือรับรองปลอดหนี้) | Budget 36 months × monthly fee |
| Tenant occupation status | 6-18 months + 200K eviction cost |
| GPS + Google Street View verification | Wrong location = wrong price |
| Renovation estimate (cap 12% of purchase) | 3,000-4,500 THB/sqm cosmetic |
| Supply pipeline (new 500+ unit projects within 1km) | Yield compression 1-2% |
| Co-living operators within 1km (> 3 = risk) | Structural demand shift |
| Summer vacancy plan (BTS or mixed tenants) | 3 months vacancy/year |
| NPA concentration in same building (cross all 6 providers) | CAM fee collapse risk |
| University/school enrollment trend (stable or growing) | Demand evaporation |

## Key Insights by Teammate

### Flipper (Buy-Renovate-Resell)
- BTS/MRT proximity trumps education anchor for exit liquidity
- 22-35 sqm sweet spot for university corridor flips
- Renovation cap at 12% of purchase price
- Need 50+ DDProperty listings in building for brand recognition
- Minimum 3 comparable transactions in 18 months
- Juristic arrears ≤ 24 months

### Rental Manager (Cashflow)
- Rent sweet spot: 5,500-9,000 THB/mo (deepest student tenant pool)
- Summer vacancy is CERTAINTY (10-12 weeks/year), not a risk
- Keycard entry is #1 non-negotiable (female student safety)
- Furnished is mandatory for university rental (+1,500-2,500/mo premium)
- Verify demand with 3 of 5 independent signals before buying
- Building age: post-2000 minimum, post-2005 preferred

### Risk Analyst (Due Diligence)
- 3 auto-reject: leasehold < 30yr, invalid title, structural notice
- Juristic fee arrears = hidden lien on unit (Section 29)
- NPA concentration > 20% = juristic fund collapse
- Eviction budget: 6-18 months + 200K THB
- Never trust provider "appraised values" — verify via กรมที่ดิน transactions
- Discount > 55% = likely hidden defects (trap, not bargain)

### Market Analyst (Future Value)
- 2008-2018 year-built sweet spot (modern enough, cheap enough)
- Orange Line (under construction) will reprice Ramkhamhaeng corridor
- Co-living operators eating 7-12K/mo student segment — favor 1BR over studio
- NPA concentration > 8% per building = managed decline signal
- Grad/professional university > undergraduate mass enrollment
- Hospital clusters near medical universities = recession-proof demand

## Education Anchor Categories

### University (student rental)
- Unit size: 22-35 sqm studio, 35-50 sqm 1BR
- Rent range: 5,500-9,000 THB/mo
- Tenant: students, young professionals
- Risk: summer vacancy, enrollment shifts

### International School (expat family rental)
- Unit size: 50-120 sqm, 2-3BR
- Rent range: 30,000-100,000+ THB/mo
- Tenant: expat families (often company housing allowance)
- Strength: year-round occupancy, long tenancy (3-15 years), price insensitive
- Risk: school bus service reduces proximity premium

### Top Thai School (Thai family rental/purchase)
- Unit size: 35-80 sqm, 1-2BR
- Rent range: 15,000-40,000 THB/mo
- Tenant: provincial families relocating for child's education
- Strength: competitive admission = parents willing to relocate
- Risk: more price sensitive than expats

## Round 1 Lessons Learned

### NPA Traps (priced AT or ABOVE market)
- Park 24 สุขุมวิท 24 (KBank) — NPA 210-261K/sqm vs market 170-221K/sqm
- The Lumpini 24 (KBank) — NPA 203K/sqm vs market 165-193K/sqm
- Clover ลาดพร้าว 83 (KBank) — NPA 73-94K/sqm vs market 67K/sqm
- Studio Zone ลาดพร้าว 102 (JAM) — NPA = market price
- The Selected เกษตร-งามวงศ์วาน (KBank) — 0% discount, 2.8% yield
- Chateau In Town พหลโยธิน 14 (BAM) — at/above market

### Leasehold Trap
- Triple Y Residence สามย่าน (JAM) — expires 2049, 23 years left. AVOID.

### Provider "Appraised Value" ≠ Real Market
- Provider discounts (e.g., "35% below appraisal") are marketing
- Only DDProperty/Hipflat actual listings and transactions matter
