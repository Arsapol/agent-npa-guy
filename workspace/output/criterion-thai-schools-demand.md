## School-by-School Analysis

### 1. เตรียมอุดมศึกษา — ปทุมวัน
- **1BR:** 17,000-30,000 THB/mo (30-55 sqm)
- **2BR:** 35,000-60,000 THB/mo (55-80 sqm)
- **Tenant:** Upcountry parents paying for child's room; some parents co-reside
- **Admission:** HIGH — national exam, tens of thousands for ~1,000 slots
- **Relocation:** STRONG — parents from เชียงใหม่, ขอนแก่น, นครราชสีมา routinely rent/buy nearby
- **Listings:** 841 active rental listings (FazWaz)
- **Insight:** Pathumwan 1BR commands 1,088 THB/sqm/month (Knight Frank 2024 — highest in Bangkok). Feeder: สาธิตจุฬาฯ → เตรียมอุดม aspiration drives demand.

### 2. สาธิตจุฬาฯ — ปทุมวัน
- **1BR:** 18,000-28,000 THB/mo
- **2BR:** 35,000-58,000 THB/mo
- **Tenant:** Affluent Thai families; K-M6 coverage = 12-year tenancies
- **Admission:** HIGH — Grade 1 and Grade 7 entrance exams
- **Relocation:** STRONG — 392 active rent listings tagged to school (ZMyHome)
- **Insight:** Co-located with เตรียมอุดม pipeline; families plan 12-year stays. Ashton Chula-Silom and Ideo Q Chula-Samyan benchmark at 25-35K/mo 1BR.

### 3. สาธิต มศว ประสานมิตร — สุขุมวิท 23
- **1BR:** 22,000-35,000 THB/mo (Asoke premium)
- **2BR:** 45,000-65,000 THB/mo
- **Tenant:** Thai upper-middle + some expats preferring Thai curriculum
- **Admission:** HIGH — Grade 1 entrance exam
- **Relocation:** STRONG — 44,650+ rental listings in catchment (PropertyHub)
- **BTS:** Asoke + MRT Sukhumvit (5-6 min walk)
- **Insight:** Best transport connectivity of all สาธิต schools; commands Sukhumvit core pricing (expat-grade returns from Thai-school demand).

### 4. สีลม Cluster (กรุงเทพคริสเตียน + เซนต์โยเซฟฯ + อัสสัมชัญ + อัสสัมชัญคอนแวนต์)
- **1BR:** 20,000-38,000 THB/mo
- **2BR:** 40,000-65,000 THB/mo
- **Tenant:** Catholic upper-class Thai families + CBD professionals
- **Relocation:** MODERATE — Bangkok-resident families, not relocators
- **Insight:** 4 top Catholic schools in one corridor + CBD employment = most diversified demand base, lowest vacancy risk. School demand is structural but secondary to CBD demand.

### 5. หอวัง — จตุจักร
- **1BR:** 13,000-23,000 THB/mo (Chatuchak avg 21,385 per Hipflat)
- **2BR:** 25,000-40,000 THB/mo
- **Tenant:** Thai middle-class; government officials
- **Relocation:** MODERATE
- **Insight:** Benefits from multiple schools + MRT/BTS Mo Chit interchange. Diversified demand, not school-specific.

### 6. บดินทรเดชา — ลาดพร้าว
- **1BR:** 9,000-15,000 THB/mo
- **2BR:** 18,000-28,000 THB/mo
- **Tenant:** Thai middle-class; MOE families
- **Relocation:** MODERATE
- **Insight:** Good volume market with affordable entry. Suitable for NPA units under 2M. Lower rent ceiling.

### 7. สาธิตเกษตร — บางเขน
- **1BR:** 9,500-14,500 THB/mo
- **2BR:** 18,000-30,000 THB/mo
- **Tenant:** Thai middle-class; KU staff
- **Relocation:** MODERATE
- **Insight:** Price-to-yield attractive — lower entry, acceptable yield if NPA under 1.5M. BTS Mo Chit connectivity helps.

### 8-11. เซนต์คาเบรียล / สตรีวิทยา / สวนกุหลาบ / ราชินี
- **Demand:** LOW to MODERATE-LOW
- **Issues:** Day-school culture (Bangkok commuters), limited condo supply in old-town areas, no strong relocation driver
- **Verdict:** Not suitable for NPA rental investment thesis

## Thai vs International School Market

| Factor | Thai Elite Schools | International Schools |
|---|---|---|
| Price sensitivity | HIGH (THB income) | LOW (expat packages/HNWI) |
| Unit size preferred | 1BR (students) or 2BR (parents) 45-80 sqm | 2-3BR 80-150 sqm |
| Furnished preference | Semi-furnished OK | Fully furnished required |
| Budget range | 12,000-40,000 THB/mo | 35,000-150,000 THB/mo |
| Rental tenure | 1-3 years (academic cycle) | 2-3 years (expat posting) |
| Relocation driver | Exam result → move if child passes | Company posting → school secondary |

## Key Investment Takeaways

1. **ปทุมวัน (เตรียมอุดม + สาธิตจุฬาฯ)** = strongest Thai-school rental market. 1,088 THB/sqm/mo for 1BR (highest in BKK). NPA 35 sqm unit → potential ฿38,080/mo rent.

2. **สุขุมวิท 23/Asoke (สาธิต มศว)** = Sukhumvit core pricing from Thai-school demand. NPA at sub-market here has strong upside.

3. **สีลม cluster** = most diversified demand (4 schools + CBD). Lowest vacancy risk.

4. **บางเขน (สาธิตเกษตร)** = value zone. Lower entry, acceptable yield if NPA < 1.5M.

5. **Avoid พระนคร (สวนกุหลาบ, ราชินี)** — no liquid condo market exists.

6. **Parent relocation demand is real but selective** — activates for national entrance exam schools (เตรียมอุดม, สาธิต series) where provincial families MUST move to Bangkok.
