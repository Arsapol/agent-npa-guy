# Ada Research Topics Catalog

Win-win research topics where NPA-guy's property analysis needs overlap with Ada's investment domain.

## 1. Thai Bank NPL & Credit Quality

**Frequency**: Quarterly (earnings season) + event-driven (oil shock, rate changes)

### What NPA-guy needs:
- Total NPL (Stage 3) balance and ratio trend
- Which banks have highest NPL growth → where foreclosures come from
- Mortgage rejection rates by segment
- NPA write-off pace → bank willingness to sell at discount

### What Ada gets:
- Bank stock picks (BBL, KBANK, SCB, KTB, TTB, KKP, CREDIT)
- Credit cycle positioning (expanding vs contracting)
- Provision trends → earnings quality signals
- Sector overweight/underweight calls

### Example brief:
```
[NPA-guy → Ada Research Request]
TOPIC: Q1 2026 Thai banking sector NPL update — all listed banks
WHY ADA CARES: Bank earnings season, credit quality drives stock picks. BBL KBANK SCB KTB TTB KKP CREDIT TIDLOR.
WHAT I NEED: NPL ratio by bank, Stage 3 loan balances, mortgage rejection rates, NPA disposal pace, provision trends
FORMAT: Per-bank table with QoQ and YoY changes
ACTION: Research + ingest to KB. I'll query for foreclosure supply forecast.
TIP: If yfinance has no data for a ticker, use your google-finance skill as fallback.
```

### Key tickers: BBL, KBANK, SCB, KTB, TTB, KKP, CREDIT, TIDLOR

## Anti-Patterns — Do NOT Ask Ada

These don't serve Ada's investment domain:
- Specific property listing details (no stock angle)
- Flood risk for a specific soi (not investable)
- Zoning regulation lookup (reference data, not research)
- Condo juristic person issues (too niche)
- Title deed verification (legal, not financial)
- Simple arithmetic (property-calc skill handles this)
